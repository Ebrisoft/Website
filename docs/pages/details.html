Why Use Habitat?

Habitat provides a streamlined mobile communication platform for landlords and tenants, with a focus on property maintenance issues.

Know the outstanding maintenance issues at a glance.

Habitat's maintenance feed will display any issues related to a property. No longer will issues be forgotten about with this consolidated issue view.

Instantly provide house information.

Never again will WiFi passwords be lost! The house pinboard can include anything; Wi-Fi passwords, bin collection days, and lockbox combinations.

One-Click Contact

Emergency at hand? Contact who you need to in seconds with Habitat's built in contact book, while separating your personal phoneboook and your tenancy phonebook.

Habitat For Landlords

Easily access all of your current property maintenance issues in one feed, allowing you to plan your days accordingly.

Habitat's issue feed allows you to view & prioritise issues submitted by tenants, ensuring urgent issues are not forgotten about. Is an issue resolved? Simply mark the issue as resolved to remove it from the main view. Don't worry though - If you need to view it again it will always be available under the 'Resolved' issue-feed filter.

Focusing on a particular property? By using the house-select button, easily filter out other house's issues - allowig you to focus on the tasks at hand.

By using the Habitat platform, you iteract with a 'house' - allowing Habitat to notify the idividuals. This allows tenants to be notified much quicker, and means you do not have to actively have to remember who lives in what house!

We recognise that Habitat wont be able to replace all tenant-landlord communication. That is why we have chosen to include a built-in contacts page. Instantly access who you need to by phone or email with one-click.

By separating your tenant contacts into Habitat, you reduce clutter and non-personal contacts from building up in your device's contact book.

Habitat For Tenants

What's the best way to contact your landlord? Email? Phone? Text? WhatsApp?

Remove confusion with Habitat. Habitat provides a single channel of communication, dedicated to facilitating communication during your tenancy. No longer have to consider what your landlord prefers, or what you think will have the problem resolved fastest.

All house issues are visible to other tenants in the house - preventing any duplicate / assumed landlord communication. Maintain openness and honesty with Habitat's house-focused approach.

All maintenance issues remain in the issue feed until they are marked as resolved. Even if an issue has been resolved, you can always access it & re-open if necessary. Using Habitat provides a platform where issues will not get buried in your landlords phone under personal life / unrelated content.
