Habitat

A multiple occupancy communication platform for landlords and tenants that establishes a professional working environment and relationship throughout a tenancy. Users can post house and maintenance issues, upload images, comment, and resolve problems, all from a single contained environment. Additional features include a pin-board for storing house information, both parties having the ability to one-click call and email one another from within the app, removing the need for cluttered address books. As well as, a calendar/schedule for tenant and landlord and push notifications for updates and announcements.
